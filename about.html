About
The
Sustainabilitist
 

Contained on these pages are the ideas, words, and images I have created thus far in my explorations of where the “Sustainabilitist” and the “Designer” in me meet. Both are mindests, philosophies, and ways of life. This site will be in a state of growth, evolution, and flux in accordance with its own principles.

There have been many related projects: betterlivingthroughsustainability.com, designsustainably.com, and other printed and digital works, posters, and publications. However, this has been the enduring result — the principles and some of the explanations and visuals. The other URLs will point here, and other writings and visuals will eventually find homes here too.

The Sustainabilitist arose from a prompt I asked myself: “What does sustainable Graphic Design look like?” From that initial question, many investigations and subsequent questions, paths, failures, and successes were had.
